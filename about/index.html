About Me

Hi there, thanks for visiting my page.

I am Muyeol Jung from South Korea and PhD student in Department of Geography, Durham University where I am looking at the impact of the construction of the weirs along rivers by analysing Water Quality Data and Diatom Data. Regarding this, more informations can be found in Research Tab.

The South Korea born and raised achieved BA and MA degrees on Geography in Kyungpook National University before I came to England for PhD Course in Durham. Between Master and PhD courses, I was involved with different types of projects funded by the government affiliated institutions.
