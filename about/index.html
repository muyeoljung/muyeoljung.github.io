About Me

Hi there, thanks for visiting my page. I am Muyeol Jung from South Korea and PhD student in Department of Geography, Durham University where I am working on Application of Diatom Analysis in Water Quality Assessment. Regarding this, more informations about this project can be found in Research Tab.

I, born and raised in Korea, achieved BA and MA degrees on Geography in Kyungpook National University before I came to England for PhD Course in Durham. Between Master and PhD courses, I was involved with different types of projects on National Park Survey, and Natural Environment Survey.
