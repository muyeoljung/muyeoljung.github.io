About Me

Hello! Thanks for visiting my page.

I am Muyeol Jung from South Korea and PhD student at the Department of Geography, Durham University where I am looking at the impact of the construction of the barrages along rivers on water quality and diatom assemblages. Regarding this, more information can be found in the Research Tab.

I achieved Bachelor's degree on Geography and Master's degree on Physical Geography at Geography department in Kyungpook National University before I moved abroad to pursue a PhD degree in Durham. Between Master and PhD courses, I was involved with different types of research projects funded by the government-affiliated institutions.
